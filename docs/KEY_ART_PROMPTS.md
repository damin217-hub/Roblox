# Key Art Prompts

These production prompts were used for the launch-art direction. Final exported files should be recorded in
`ASSET_REGISTER.md` before upload.

## 512×512 experience icon

Create a polished, cheerful square icon for an original Roblox drawing-and-guessing party game called
Momo's Art Academy. Show a friendly original blocky curator mascot in a pink beret holding a glowing
paintbrush toward a white canvas with a simple cat doodle. Include two excited original blocky students,
a bright circular art academy, and violet, coral, cyan and yellow paint splashes. Use premium toy-like
3D game key art with a tight centered composition readable at small size. No text, logos, copyrighted
characters, watermark, weapons, or realistic people.

## 1920×1080 thumbnail

Create wide cinematic promotional key art for Momo's Art Academy. Show a grand circular purple-and-gold
art academy arena, a glowing central canvas, curator Momo in a pink beret presenting a funny cat drawing,
and a diverse group of original blocky players drawing, guessing and celebrating. Use colorful premium
3D party-game advertising art with clear silhouettes and uncluttered upper-left space for optional title
placement. No text, Roblox logo, copyrighted characters, watermark, weapons, or realistic people.
